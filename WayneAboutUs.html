History

Wayne Enterprises was founded by Judge Solomon and Joshua Wayne in the 17th century however it became corporate company until the 19th century
when son and heir Alan Wayne came into power. Energized by the world's Industrial Revolution; more branches were created and diversified while
others dwindled and were discarded. Wayne Enterprises became a "green company" and environmentally conscious when Patrick and Laura Wayne came
into power thus leaving an evergrowing company to Thomas and Martha Wayne. However, their son Bruce Wayne would soon after inherit the family
fortune as a result of the murder of the past owners.
