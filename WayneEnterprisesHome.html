What we do

For generations Wayne Industries has strived to enhance the quality of life in Gotham by working in partnership with the Gotham City Police Department to enforce the law and preserve peace. Our various divisions provide citizens with resources and services necessary to thrive here in Gotham.

In recent years, Wayne Technologies has had large breakthroughs in medicine, cybernetics, pharmaceuticals and avionics. This division has played an essential role in providing the latest and cutting edge technology to improve the lives of millions of citizens.

At Wayne Industries our team researches, and develops cleaner, mechanical fission and fusion power plants; and also run many factories and normal labor units. Our contributions in the industrial circuit can be seen through the development of industrial machinery such as the manufacturing of heavy engines, motors, pneumatic systems and large scale systems.



Wayne Medical is a prominent division with vast fields of study and work. We provide the highest quality healthcare system in Gotham and uphold many hospitals and orphanages. At Wayne Medical our staff focus on researching and treating illnesses such as cancer and AIDS in partership with Wayne Biotech.
